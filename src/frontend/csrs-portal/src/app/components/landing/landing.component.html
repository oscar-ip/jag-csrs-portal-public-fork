Child Support Recalculation Service
The Child Support Recalculation Service helps families with child support orders and written agreements maintain a fair standard of support for their children by reviewing the amount of child support each year. The recalculation service adjusts the amount of child support if income has gone up or down so that parents do not have to return to court to have their child support amounts reviewed. Learn more >

Is the recalculation service right for you?

Complete the questionnaire to obtain important information about eligibility for the service to help you decide whether to apply.

Please note, the recalculation process takes approximately 4 to 5 months to complete.
